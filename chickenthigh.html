Skillet Chicken Thighs with Spring Vegetables and Shallot Vinaigrette

Serving Size

10 serving

About

This elegant single-skillet meal for two is deceptively easy to make. Ready in under an hour, this skillet chicken dinner includes your protein (perfectly golden-crisp chicken thighs), green veggies (peas and asparagus), a starch (baby Yukon gold potatoes),

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 (6-oz.) bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • ½ teaspoon table salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup baby Yukon Gold potatoes, halved (about 3/4 lb.)
  • 1 ½ cups fresh asparagus, cut in 1-in. pieces
