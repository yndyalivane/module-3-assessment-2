Serving Size

Serves 2 as a starter

About

Plump prawns in a deliciously garlicky, spicy oil – this easy recipe is perfect for entertaining. Don’t forget the crusty bread for mopping up all of those wonderful juices

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 180g pack peeled raw king prawns
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 2 tsp sweet smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • a handful flat-leaf parsley, chopped
  • ½ lemon, wedged to serve
